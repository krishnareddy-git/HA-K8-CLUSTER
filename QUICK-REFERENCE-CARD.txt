üéØ IF ASKED TECHNICAL QUESTIONS

A: Smallest unit, 1+ containers sharing network and storage

Q: What is a Deployment?
A: Manages pods, provides scaling, updates, self-healing

Q: What is a Service?
A: Stable network endpoint, load balances traffic to pods

A: Distributed key-value store, cluster's brain/database
